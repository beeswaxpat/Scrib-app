## Why Scrib?

Most notes apps require accounts, sync to the cloud, and track your behavior. Scrib does none of that. It's a simple, fast, offline notepad that keeps your data on your phone where it belongs.

**Perfect for:** privacy-conscious users, journalists, students, anyone who wants notes without the bloat.

## Features

- **100% Offline** — Notes stay on your device, no internet required
- **Zero Data Collection** — No accounts, no tracking, no analytics
- **Auto-Save** — Never lose your thoughts
- **Voice Input** — Dictate notes hands-free
- **Color Coding** — Organize with 8 note colors and 5 accent themes
- **Dark Mode** — System, light, or dark theme
- **Bullet Lists** — One-tap bullet mode for quick lists
- **Full Accessibility** — TalkBack support, adjustable text sizes
- **Export to TXT** — Take your notes anywhere
- **10 Languages** — English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Russian
